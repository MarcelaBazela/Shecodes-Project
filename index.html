Sourdough Bread Sourdough can be defined as an intermediate product containing a flour/water mixture that has been fermented by a combination of bacteria, usually heterofermentative strains of lactic acid bacteria and yeast (DeVuyst and Neysens, 2005), resulting in an end product with a sour taste.
